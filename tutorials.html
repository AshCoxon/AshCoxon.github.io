The tutorials shown on this page are for mixed level cardists, descriptions are in the information below the video. For more advanced cardistry flourishes refer to the YouTube pages of the cardists listed in the about section. Don't be disheartened if you can't do any flourish straight away, none of these moves can be learned instantly. Some moves shown may have to be adapted slightly due to differing hand sizes.
